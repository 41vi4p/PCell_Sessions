About the Department

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (AI & Data Science) focuses on strong computing fundamentals while equipping students with skills in Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and emerging technologies, emphasizing analytical thinking and practical learning.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To develop competent, innovative, and ethical engineers who contribute meaningfully to society and the global technology ecosystem.

Mission

To provide quality technical education, promote continuous learning and research, and nurture professional excellence in emerging technologies.
